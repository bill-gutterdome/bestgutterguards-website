Dealer FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about partnering with GutterDome

How much does it cost to become a dealer?

No licensing fees or franchise costs. You simply purchase product at wholesale pricing. We believe in earning your business through great products and support, not extracting fees.

Do I get exclusive territory rights?

Yes. All active dealers receive semi-exclusive protected territory in their Designated Market Area (DMA). We don't compete against our own dealers—your success is our success.

How fast can I get product delivered?

We guarantee shipment within 2 business days or it's free. No more waiting 2-6 weeks for backorders. Your customers and employees depend on timely delivery—we get it.

Do you provide leads or do I find my own customers?

Both. All corporate-generated consumer leads in your territory are emailed to you free of charge. You can also generate your own leads—we support your existing marketing efforts with 5% co-op advertising dollars.

Do I need gutter installation experience?

Experience helps but isn't required. We provide professional installation training, technical support, and all the sales tools you need. Many successful dealers came from roofing, siding, or general contracting backgrounds.

What's the profit potential?

Most dealers add $50,000+ annually with minimal overhead. We offer highly competitive tiered pricing with volume discounts and truckload pricing for larger operations. Plus quarterly promotions to boost your margins.
